Cowboy's Bluff
==============
**Authors:** *Madison Sellers, Kel Raphael*

***A classic card game, with a morbid twist.***


You've been wandering a while, and found yourself a nice saloon. It's a pleasant respite from the hot sun... except for the rival cowboy you find messing around in your territory. You decide to settle it all peaceful-like, though, with a simple game of Go Fish.

And if he decides to cheat, well. That's what your trusty revolver is for.

## Instructions

The goal of the game is to place matching sets of cards. 

On your turn, you will ask your opponent for one of the cards in your hand. If they have one of those cards, they have to give it to you (or cheat. We'll get to that later.). In this situation, you get to ask again for another card.

When you ask for a card that they don't have ('Go fish'), you draw a random card, and your turn ends.

## Cheating

While you might be a fine cowboy, you don't mind a little cheating now and then. When you're doing it, of course. 

You can take an extra card at the end of your turn. Or, when asked for a card, you could be less than truthful about whether you have it. 

Be careful, though. If your opponent catches on, they won't be very happy. And keep an eye out yourself; you never know what your opponent could do.
